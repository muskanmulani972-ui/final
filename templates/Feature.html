System Features

Real-Time Entry & Exit Tracking

Records student entry and exit activities in real time with accurate date and time, ensuring reliable monitoring of campus movement.

Secure Admin Access

Provides secure login for authorized administrators to manage and monitor student entry and exit records effectively.

Student Movement History

Maintains a complete digital history of student movements, enabling easy retrieval and verification of past records.

Centralized Monitoring Dashboard

Offers a centralized interface for administrators to monitor all entry and exit activities across the campus.

Enhanced Campus Security

Helps identify unauthorized or irregular movements, contributing to improved safety and security within the institution.

Automated Record Management

Eliminates manual registers by maintaining digital records, reducing paperwork and minimizing human errors.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed with a simple and intuitive interface, allowing administrators to operate the system with minimal training.

Scalable System Design

Built with scalability in mind, allowing future enhancements and integration with additional campus management modules.
